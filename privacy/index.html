Privacy policy

This static travel guide does not use analytics, advertising trackers, contact forms, account features, cookies, or non-essential browser storage. Locale and airport choices stay in the URL so they can be shared without storing a preference on the device.

Data processed to serve the site

The site is hosted by Vercel. Like most hosting providers, Vercel may process standard technical request data such as IP address, user agent, requested URL, timestamp, and basic security logs to deliver the page, prevent abuse, and operate the hosting service.

External links

Links to DB Navigator, ÖBB, Google Maps, GitHub, and other resources leave this site. Those services process data under their own policies.

Contact and rights

For privacy or security questions, use the public project repository. Visitors in jurisdictions with privacy rights may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, or objection where applicable.
